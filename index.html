Welcome!

Hi, I’m Anora Anish — a first-year undergraduate student at Christ College, Irinjalakuda, Kerala, pursuing a Bachelor's in Physics with a minor in Mathematics.

My journey so far has taken me through many explorations — from science and writing to introspection and curiosity. I believe learning doesn’t just happen in classrooms but also in the quiet moments of doubt, reflection, and discovery.

I started this space to archive my thoughts, questions, and experiments — both academic and personal. Whether you’re a fellow student, a wanderer like me, or simply curious — I hope something here resonates with you.

In my free time, I love exploring coding, reading philosophy, and journaling thoughts that don’t always have answers — but deserve to be voiced.

Thank you for stopping by.
