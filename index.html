👋 Hello, I’m Anora Anish

I’m a first-year undergraduate student at Christ College, Irinjalakuda, Kerala, currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Physics with Mathematics as my minor.

My academic journey has been anything but linear. From navigating competitive entrances to the experience of applying (and not getting in) to MIT, I’ve encountered my share of setbacks — but each of them shaped me. These experiences have taught me that academics isn’t only about achievements or perfect grades. It’s also about resilience, reflection, and how we transform obstacles into motivation. I hope to share that with you. 🌱
