✍️ My Blog

Writing is my way of thinking out loud — of turning quiet reflections into something shareable. My blog is divided into three main sections:

1. Academic Blog

Here, I share personal insights from my academic life — not just what I’ve learned from books, but also what I’ve learned from failure, confusion, and recovery. These are stories of struggle and small triumphs, sometimes philosophical, often sincere — and always written with the hope that someone, somewhere, might find them relatable.

2. Personal Writings (Malayalam)

Some thoughts come best in your mother tongue. This space is reserved for my personal reflections written in Malayalam, capturing emotions and ideas that feel more authentic in the language I grew up with.
