About Me

I’m Anora Anish, a B.Sc. Physics undergraduate at Christ College, Thrissur. My academic curiosity spans classical mechanics, quantum physics, and the beauty of scientific inquiry — but it doesn’t stop there. I also enjoy exploring the intersection between science, writing, and public engagement.

Beyond the classroom, I write to reflect, question, and connect — often in Malayalam and often philosophically. I believe storytelling, whether scientific or personal, is a way of empowering minds.

I actively participate in research-oriented programs and fellowships and aim to contribute to science communication and outreach in ways that are creative, inclusive, and impactful.

This website brings together my academic interests, personal writings, and useful resources — not just as a portfolio, but as a shared space for ideas to grow.
